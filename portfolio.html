Performance is not a feature; it's a requirement. I focus on optimizing asset delivery, minimizing render-blocking resources, and writing efficient JavaScript to ensure fast loading times and a smooth 60fps interaction experience.

I prioritize code health through strict component architecture, proper typing, and comprehensive unit testing. This ensures the codebase remains maintainable, scalable, and resilient as the product grows.

Before I stepped fully into web development, I worked as a virtual assistant. A lot of my early tasks involved handling emails, content, and small technical fixes — long before I knew I'd fall in love with writing real code. Those moments were my first glimpse into how the web actually works.

As I grew more curious, I began studying web development and building projects of my own. From recreating classic games like the Simon Game to experimenting with UI layouts and Tailwind, every project has pushed me to understand not just how things look, but how they function behind the scenes. Now, my learning journey is driven by the excitement of exploring new languages, new tools, and new challenges.

I believe in building in public, documenting my process, and sharing what I learn — even the messy parts. That's how I grow, and hopefully how I can help others grow too.
